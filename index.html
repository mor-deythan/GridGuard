⚡ How to Play
🎯 Objective
Work together to guide the energy grid through a full loop of the board. Answer questions correctly to keep your Economy, Environment, and Society balanced — and build up enough EU Counter to win!

🔄 Turn Flow
1. Land on a tile — click the glowing tile to activate it.
2. Answer a question — choose the best answer about that energy source.
3. Earn rewards — correct answers boost your metrics and EU Counter.
4. Challenge tiles — present an industry scenario with 3 options; each affects metrics differently.
5. Crisis tiles — answer 3 emergency questions correctly to avoid heavy penalties.
📊 Metrics
💰 Economy Reflects the industrial and economic health of your society. Strong economy enables investment in clean energy infrastructure and resilient systems.
🌿 Environment CO₂ impact and ecological balance. Don't let it collapse!
👥 Society Public acceptance and wellbeing of communities.
⚡ Energy Units Your resource for tackling challenges — spend them wisely. You can't keep them high!
🏆 Winning & Losing
Complete a full loop of the board. Energy Units must be spent on challenges along the way — don't hoard them! At the end:
Victory — all three metrics (Economy, Environment, Society) are above 40.
Partial — all three metrics are above 30, but at least one hasn't reached 40.
Defeat — any metric drops to 30 or below.
